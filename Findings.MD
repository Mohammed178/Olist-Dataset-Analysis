## Late delivery rate (monthly)
- Late rate worsens over time as order volume grows
- Worst month: Mar 2018 at 20.75% (1 in 5 orders late)
- Feb–Mar 2018 cluster (15–21%) suggests sustained operational strain, not a blip
- Order volume ~2.7x from Mar 2017 to Mar 2018 — capacity didn't scale with demand
- Hypothesis upgraded: late delivery is a structural problem, not an anomaly
## Review score: late vs on-time
- Late orders average 2.57 stars vs 4.29 for on-time
- 1.72-star penalty — late delivery flips a "great" order into a "poor" one
- 7,701 late orders (~8%) vs 88,658 on-time — robust sample
